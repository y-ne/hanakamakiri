### Support

For support, some of the filament actually got fused onto the actual model, this is what i do for the `Z Top Distance`.

-- i use `.X6 mm` or `.X8 mm` first. what does this mean is, if you print with `0.16 mm layer height`. u use `0.18 mm` or `0.16 mm` for the `Top Z Distance`.

-- some folk use `0.28 mm` as their general ( i personally never try this ).

-- rule of thumb is you just multiple your `layer height` by `2`. so if you print `0.14 mm` you set `Top Z Distance` with `0.28 mm`. (never try this too, maybe sometime).
